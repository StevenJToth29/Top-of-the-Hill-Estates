# Design Schema: Top of the Hill Rooms

## 1. Brand Identity & Visual North Star
**Core Concept:** "The Midnight Curator"
A premium, atmospheric experience that feels like a private desert sanctuary at night. The design balances raw Arizona beauty with sophisticated modern luxury.

## 2. Design Tokens
### Color Palette
*   **Deep Onyx (#0D1B2A):** The primary background color, providing a moody, architectural foundation.
*   **Brand Teal (#2DD4BF):** Our signature accent. Used for primary CTAs, active states, and high-impact UI highlights.
*   **Slate Silvers (#94A3B8 / #E2E8F0):** For secondary text and subtle borders to maintain high legibility against dark backgrounds.
*   **Starlight White (#F8FAFC):** Reserved for primary headings and critical interface labels.

### Typography (Manrope & Plus Jakarta Sans)
*   **Headlines:** Manrope (Extra Bold). Modern, slightly technical, and high-impact.
*   **Subheadings/Labels:** Plus Jakarta Sans (Medium/Bold). Clean and highly legible for functional data.
*   **Body:** Plus Jakarta Sans (Regular). Optimized for reading property descriptions and guest details.

### Shape & Spacing
*   **Corner Radius:** 8px (Round Eight). Strikes a balance between organic softness and architectural precision.
*   **Glassmorphism:** Semi-transparent panels with a 40-60% opacity background blur. Subtle white/teal borders (1px) to create the "floating" effect.

## 3. Component Guidelines
*   **Navigation Bar:** Fixed, glassmorphic blur. Logo prominent on the left. Navigation links with teal active states.
*   **Search Interface:** "Floating" glass panel. Minimalist input fields with high-contrast labels. Teal action buttons for room discovery.
*   **Property Cards:** Image-centric. Glass overlays for pricing and status. Subtle hover elevations.
*   **Buttons:**
    *   *Primary:* Solid Teal background with Dark Onyx text. High contrast for clear conversion.
    *   *Secondary/Outline:* 1px Teal or Slate border with transparent background.

## 4. Imagery & Mood
*   **Atmosphere:** Low-light photography with warm interior glows.
*   **Theme:** Focused on intimate "Sanctuaries" (single bedrooms/suites) and dramatic Arizona nightscapes.
*   **Texture:** Subtle gradients and "starlight" noise patterns to add depth to dark surfaces.
